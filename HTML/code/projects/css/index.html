PRABHAS

(Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju)

This is a name that needs no introduction to the world of cinema.

About

Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju born on (23 October 1979), known mononymously as Prabhas, is an Indian actor who predominantly works in Telugu cinema. Referred to in the media as the "Rebel Star", he is one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema. Prabhas has been featuring in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2015 and has received seven Filmfare Awards nominations, a Nandi Award, and a SIIMA Award.
